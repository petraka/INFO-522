"Whatever the cost of our libraries, the price is cheap compared to that of an ignorant nation." ~ Walter Cronkite (1995)

"Google can bring you back 100,000 answers, a librarian can bring you back the right one." ~ Neil Gaiman (2010)

I believe that everyone deserves access to information and opportunity to develop their information literacy skills. I want to be a part of creating environments and relationships that encourage the achievement of these ideals. To better help my patrons, I also attempt to continually grow my personal skills and familiarity with technologies and information offerings.
