About Me

I'm born and raised in Pittsburgh, PA, but I'm almost positive I will end up moving for work at some point in my life. I've come to the information sciences profession in a roundabout way, but it has been incredibly fulfilling thus far and I'm looking forward to continuing to grow in the field.

I don't have much free time between working full-time and being in grad school, but when I'm not working on one thing or another, I enjoy watching period piece dramas, doing puzzles of all kinds, visiting new places (especially coffee shops), and, of course, reading. Recently, I've also gotten into mechanical keyboards. I'm still very much a novice, but it's been a lot of fun trying to decide what my optimal build might be.
