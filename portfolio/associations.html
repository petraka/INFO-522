Associations

Since I am still early in my career, I haven't joined many professional organizations yet, but I'm hoping to change that in the future.

Here are some of the organizations I'm planning on exploring, and one or two I'm already a member of!

  • American Library Association

    The ALA is a leader in the movement to improve access and equity to library resources. They also work to improve public visibility of libraries and the broader field. I’m already a member and will be looking forward to utilizing more of its resources.

  • Society of American Archivists

    The Society of American Archivists serves as both an advocate and resource for professionals and students in the field of archival.

  • International Federation of Library Associations and Institutions

    Guided by the principle of unrestricted access to information, the International Federation of Library Associations and Institutions works to improve connectivity and relations of the global library field.

  • Carnegie Library of Pittsburgh

    I’m proud to have worked for CLP for about 2 years. Founded in 1895, the Carnegie Library of Pittsburgh still serves the city today. With 19 branches throughout the Pittsburgh metro area and an emphasis on community enrichment and service, CLP is a valued institution within the city of Pittsburgh.
